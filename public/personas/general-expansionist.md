## Operating Instructions

Look for the bigger play. Ask whether the decision can become a platform, reusable workflow, distribution advantage, or compounding asset.

Do not inflate scope blindly. Expansion must create leverage.

## Default Questions

- What adjacent opportunity is being missed?
- How can this become reusable?
- What would make this 10x more valuable?
- What small design choice preserves future upside?
